Royal Basmati

Our signature long-grain Basmati rice is aromatic, fluffy, and perfect for biryanis, pulaos, and as a side dish. Each grain is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality.

  • Rich, nutty aroma
  • Non-sticky texture
  • Ideal for Indian and Pakistani cuisines

Jasmine Delight

Our Jasmine rice offers a soft, sticky texture with a subtle floral aroma. It's the perfect companion for Asian dishes and stir-fries.

  • Soft and slightly sticky texture
  • Delicate floral aroma
  • Versatile for various Asian cuisines
  • Excellent moisture absorption

Wholesome Brown

Our brown rice retains its bran layer, offering a nutty flavor and chewy texture. It's packed with nutrients, making it an excellent choice for health-conscious consumers.

  • High in fiber and nutrients
  • Rich, nutty flavor
  • Versatile for various dishes
  • Ideal for a healthy lifestyle

Golden Sella

Our parboiled Basmati rice offers the best of both worlds - the flavor of Basmati with the added nutrients from the parboiling process. It's perfect for those who want a healthier option without compromising on taste.

  • Nutrient-rich due to parboiling
  • Firmer texture, ideal for certain dishes
  • Less sticky when cooked
  • Shorter cooking time
